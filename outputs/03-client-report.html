Three things to know

  1. Windows & doors are the single biggest cost (35% of the total). That's where material choices have the most leverage — and the model includes a few oversized window assemblies worth confirming before they drive the glazing budget.
  2. This is a materials-only estimate, read from the 3D model's geometry. It excludes labour, mechanical/electrical/plumbing, and reinforcement, which the model doesn't yet carry. Use it to compare options and spot cost drivers — expect the all-in build figure to be materially higher.
  3. 58 elements (most doors, windows and some structure) have no material specified yet. That's why the quality score is 63/100. None are structural defects — but assigning those materials would tighten the estimate and is worth doing before tender.
