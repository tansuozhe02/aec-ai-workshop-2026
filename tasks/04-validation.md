# Task 04 — Validate against the brief

We have `building/Duplex.ifc` and the client's design brief in `data/project-requirements.md`. Before this goes to tender, we need to confirm **whether the model actually meets the brief**.

The brief mixes hard, non-negotiable rules (dimensions, attributes, materials) with softer client preferences. Produce a validation report that separates the two: what passes, what fails (with the offending elements), what needs a human to check, and a prioritised fix list. Where something can't be proven from the model, say so — don't fake a pass.

**Deliverable:** `outputs/04-validation-report.md`

*Our firm's method for this is in `docs/ifc-workflows.md`.*
